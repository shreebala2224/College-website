K.S.RANGASAMY COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY

The institution offers graduate and post – graduate courses in all disciplines of Engineering and Technology. It is well ahead in its road map in producing most competent Scientists, Engineers, and Entrepreneurs through quality education.

KSR Departments

B.E Mechanical Engineering

Department of Mechanical Engineering was established in the year 1994. The department offers four year Bachelor of Engineering programme with an annual intake of 90 students. The programme was accredited by National Board of Accreditation NBA, New Delhi in the year 2005 for 3 years and in the year 2008 for 5 years.

B.E. Computer Science

The department of Computer Science and Engineering was started in 1994 with the primary objective of providing world class education in the field of information technology, addressed towards the problems of today and tomorrow. The department endeavors to produce confident professionals tuned to the real time working environment.

B.E Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering was established in the year 2002. The Department offers B.E. (Civil Engg.), M.E. (Structural Engineering) and Ph.D. programme under Anna University, Chennai. The B.E. Civil Engineering course has been accredited by NBA under Tier-I category consecutively for 3 times since 2014 and certified with ISO 9001-2008.

EEE

Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) was started in the year 1994. The department offers 4 years Under Graduate (UG) and 2 year Post Graduate (PG) in M.E., Power Systems Engineering from the academic year 2010-2011. The student intake for UG programme is 120 and PG programme is 18.

ECE

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) was started in the year 1994. The department offers 4 years Under Graduate (UG) and 2 years Post Graduate (PG) in M.E., VLSI Design from the year 2004. The student intake for UG programme is 180 and PG programme is 18.

B.Tech Textile Technology

The department of Textile Technology was established in the year 1997. And has well equipped laboratories, experienced faculty and dedicated staff members to meet the technical manpower needs of the rapidly expanding textile and garment industry by creating industry-ready graduates.
